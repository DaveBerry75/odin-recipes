Chilli Con Carne

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbs oil
  • 1 teaspoon hot chilli powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Can chopped tomatoes
  • 500g minced beef
  • Beef stock
  • Half tsp dried marjoram
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • Can red kidney beans
  • Soured cream

Steps

  1. Cook onion in oil on mediem heat
  2. Add garlic, chopped pepper and spices and cook for 5 mins
  3. Brown the beef
  4. Add beef stock to the pan
  5. Add tomatoes, sugar and salt and pepper
  6. Add tomato puree
  7. Simmer for 20 mins
  8. Add kidney beans, turn up heat and bubble for 10 mins
  9. Season as necessary, put on lid, turn off heat and leave to stand for 10 mins
  10. Serve with soured cream and rice
